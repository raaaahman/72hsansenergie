Oppressiv'light

In this game, you are in the DARK. You are a robot trapped in an hostile spaceship with an almost dead battery and your only source of power is light. To survive, you will have to activate all levers in all levels to fully restore the lights on the ship. But this would be too easy If there werent an evil creature lurking in the shadows. Your only weapon is the powerful lightbulb on your head. Good luck, and pray god to not run out of power ! Controls: Left-click to activate the flashlight Arrows to move your robot
